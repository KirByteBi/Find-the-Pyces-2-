Discover Bitlands

In Find the Pyces (2), you will explore Bitlands, where you'll discover exciting secrets like the city graffiti, explore mysterious caves and the active volcano of this world, or go shopping and unlock exclusive Pyces from the prequel! Bitlands invites you to discover its content.

In this world, you'll have a rare tablet at your disposal called Pixible.

What does it do? On this tablet, you'll collect data from the Pyces you interact with and discover their hidden secrets. You can also see their difficulty and a hint to find them. The most difficult ones will have a burning mark, so keep your eyes peeled.

Players transform into "Travelers" who investigate the Pyces and will gradually discover that this world isn't perfect. Bitlands isn't all harmony, as there are dark secrets and horrors in these lands.

That being said... Are you ready to explore Bitlands?
