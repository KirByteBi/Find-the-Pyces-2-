What is a Pyce?

Pyces are entities that lived a few years before the discovery of Bitlands in 2026.

Originally they were computers that updated for the new world, now being blue in color with a dark blue screen. They are happier than their non-updated counterparts.

Pyces, as is well known, have a mouse as a hand and a broken keyboard. These have been modified to fit the new aesthetic as well.

These beings that inhabit Bitlands plan to rebuild the civilization that once inhabited this place, before the incident that made humanity evaporate from the new world.

Pyces are not evil, and their different personalities and aesthetics are products of a factory called PixelStar Studios. However, not all Pyces are peaceful — some seek to create conflicts. So each one is different from the last.
