Portalogy and Bitcore

What is Portalogy?

Portalogy was discovered in Techspawn as a power that kept Pyces always charged and active. It doesn't harm, but it's like the air all humans breathe: always active and necessary.

It also came to be called Portalogy as the study of portals to take humans from the main planet (Techspawn) to its other satellites. For example, MoltenFrost Constellation was one of these, abbreviated as MFC or MoltenFrost Cons.

Portalogy in Bitlands

In Bitlands, something different happens. It follows the same logic as Techspawn, but now it has been applied to more things:

  • 🥚 Easter eggs — that Pyces create for Easter
  • 🌀 Teleport orbs — to make them work and connect zones
  • 🚂 Environment structures — like the minecarts in Volcanic Cave

Updated Portalogy has many uses in Bitlands, integrated into the daily life of Pyces.

Bitcore: The Nuclear Power

Bitcore is the core power of Bitlands, an ancestral power very similar to Portalogy, but much more powerful.
