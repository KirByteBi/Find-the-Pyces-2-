Bitlands: The Main World

History of Bitlands

Bitlands is a magical world where humans lived for a long time. These beings sought a power, what is known today as Bitcore, the ancestral and vital power of Bitlands. With it, they tried to create a prototype for what would eventually become the Pyces, but something happened: this prototype had a failure, a failure that would be fatal for humans.

When this failure occurred, all humans were expelled from Bitlands by an explosion and sent to different dimensions, where they could never return... This prototype is now known as KirByte_Bi, or Kirb to friends.

The researcher who carried out the prototype and all the investigations, along with the creation of Pixible, is called Corein Bytech. In present-day Bitlands, you can still find his writings that reveal his name and some projects or explanations of the Pyces he wanted to create and could... Meet?

Bitlands Zones

🌲 GreenWare

The first world you visit and the center of Bitlands. It has snowy mountains with secrets, meadows, and bridges that connect everything.

Subzone - The Shop: Here you can get Pyces from the prequel (with their respective emblems) or buy Pyces with your Robux, which apparently have a higher value than the PyCoins that Pyces use to negotiate or buy.

🏙️ City

A wide city with a two-story parking lot that was destroyed after the prototype explosion. It is said that Corein Bytech did the experiment there, which is why the second floor is destroyed. However, Pyces love it, as it's material for graffiti and parkour through the buildings and cranes that make this place an active zone.

🌋 Volcano

The burning volcano and its caves are a very necessary attraction for this world. The exterior of the volcano is scalable and not easy, as the abundant lava and restless floors make climbing a challenge. The Pyces that inhabit the exterior are strange: some are volcanic materials, others are gems...

Subzone - The Volcanic Cave: It's at a corner of the volcano, where Pyces turned into gems and miners live. It is said that gems are a necessary material for balance, and that's where Eternium (gem of eternity) and Paradoxium (gem of time) come into play.

💧 UnderWater (UW)

Humans wanted to be able to breathe underwater without drowning, so Corein devised a bottle, made it giant, and put it in the water.

Now it's a place where "Pysh" (Pyce + Fish) live adapted to this environment. These Pyces are adapted to water, because conventional ones would inevitably shut down. The bottle environment holds ruined ruins that could have been something.

The entrance to this zone from GreenWare also enters the "UW" zone, being a small beach for fishing and where there are sandcastles, because not all Pyces like to swim.

Exploring Bitlands

Teleport orb between zones

When you start playing, you'll be in GreenWare. Throughout it, there are portals known as orbs that, when touched, will take you to other zones. Although you can also use the well-known Fast Travel button next to the button to open Pixible, very practical for when something becomes repetitive.

✨ We recommend using the orbs to not leave any place unexplored, as you might miss Pyces or secret spots if you don't visit those places.
